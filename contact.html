Public-sector organisations operate in environments where decisions must be transparent, defensible, and able to withstand scrutiny. Whether you're navigating a complex procurement, preparing for an audit, or seeking independent assurance over a high-risk commercial decision, Foxbane Consulting is here to help.

We work with agencies, boards, and senior leaders who want confidence that their procurement and commercial processes are robust, well-governed, and aligned with legislative and public expectations. If you need clarity, independence, or a trusted external perspective, we're ready to support you.

Organisations reach out to us when they need:

  • Independent assurance over high-value or high-risk procurements
  • Expert guidance on commercial strategy, evaluation processes, or contract management
  • Support navigating scrutiny, including audit, media, or ministerial interest
  • Probity and conflict-of-interest advice to protect integrity and public trust
  • A clear, risk-based view of what matters and what does not
  • Confidence that decisions will stand up to challenge over time.

Whether you're seeking a one-off review, ongoing assurance support, or early advice on a new procurement or commercial initiative, Foxbane Consulting provides practical, evidence-based insights grounded in real public-sector experience.
