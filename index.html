Foxbane Consulting is an independent assurance and advisory firm supporting public-sector organisations that operate in complex, high-risk, and high-scrutiny environments. We help agencies make procurement and commercial decisions that are transparent, defensible, and aligned with public expectations of integrity and value.

Our work centres on assurance over procurement, commercial decision-making, and contract management. We partner with procurement professionals, boards, and senior leaders to provide clear, evidence-based insights that strengthen governance and support confident decision-making. Whether reviewing a high-value procurement, advising on commercial strategy, or assessing contract performance, our focus is on identifying the issues that genuinely matter, not the procedural noise that can obscure them.

What sets Foxbane Consulting apart is our combination of deep public-sector experience and a practical, risk-based approach. We understand the pressures of operating under audit, media, and ministerial scrutiny, and we tailor our advice to help organisations navigate those pressures while maintaining momentum and delivering outcomes.

We support clients to design and implement procurement and commercial arrangements that stand up over time. This includes strengthening governance frameworks, managing probity and conflicts of interest, improving documentation quality, and ensuring that procurement strategies are both robust and proportionate to risk.

At Foxbane Consulting, our commitment is simple: clarity, independence, and advice grounded in real-world public-sector experience. We help organisations make decisions that stand up to challenge, stand up over time, and stand up for the public they serve.
